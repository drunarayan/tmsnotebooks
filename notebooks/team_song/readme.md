## Team Song - What a Wonderful World - Call & Response

The team will learn to sing as a group. The selected song was sung by John Bapiste at the Kennedy Center in Washington DC with President Obama in attendance. 

The song is about appreciation of Mother Nature and giving thanks. It is also about the human condition and love for others.

The song is in a Call Response Format - Advaith will lead us and we will all folow each stanza.  Here is the video of the song we will learn to sing.
